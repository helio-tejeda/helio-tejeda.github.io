## Course Description

Artificial Intelligence is an exciting area of research, which is now finding its way into commercial applications. It has the potential to significantly change our lives. Some say for better, some say for worse.  This course will explore some of the issues associated with Artificial Intelligence, robotics, and social media. We will discuss how this technology may affect our lives. Subjects include, but not limited to:

🤖 How is AI controlling our lives?
🤖 Do we want robotic companions and intelligent assistants?
🤖 Should we regulate automated systems?
🤖 Can machines be sentient and should they have rights?

In all these subjects, we will try to separate “hype” from “reality”. We will investigate these issues through the lens of media attention, research findings, science fiction, and movies.
