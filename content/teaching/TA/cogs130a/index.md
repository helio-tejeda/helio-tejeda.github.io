## Course Description

This class will explain the nature of visual mechanisms that operate in the human mind and will provide numerous illustrations like the ones above. The underlying theories will be accompanied by the discussion of experimental results describing how adult humans see, as well as how children and infants see. This review will cover the role of binocular vision, motion, shading and texture. This class will also provide an historical perspective showing how our knowledge of perception developed during the last 1000 years.
