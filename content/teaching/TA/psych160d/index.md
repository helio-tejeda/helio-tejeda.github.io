## Course Description

This course will explore human behavior from the perspective of brain structure, function, and disease. Weekly topics will include discussion of neurological diseases, neuropsychiatric disorders, and brain lesions affecting such behaviors as sensory perception, motor control, language, memory, and higher cognitive function. Emphasis will be placed on evidence acquired from patient examples, neuroimaging, neuronal recordings and brain stimulation. We will also discuss standard and experimental treatments for many of the conditions and consider medical ethics and policy.
